Purchasing Jigloo Professional Site licenses

A Jigloo professional site license covers use by an unlimited number of developers at one geographical location, such as a city.
It also includes one year of upgrades. Additional years of upgrades can be purchased at 25% of the current site license price.

The Professional Site License is available at $2,500 USD.

You may purchase a Professional Site license or licenses using your credit card via the PayPal site.


Note for Non-US users: You can still use PayPal, but you need to open a PayPal account to do so. To open an account, you can go to the following page on the PayPal site: https://www.paypal.com/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=_international-register and then come back to this page to purchase a license.
