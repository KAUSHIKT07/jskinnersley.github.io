Purchasing Multiple Professional licenses

Discounted Purchase (10%)

The Professional License is available at a 10% discount for bulk purchases (which works out to be $67.50 per single-user license).

You may purchase multiple Professional Licenses at this discounted rate using your credit card via the PayPal site.


Note for Non-US users: You can still use PayPal, but you need to open a PayPal account to do so. To open an account, you can go to the following page on the PayPal site: https://www.paypal.com/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=_international-register and then come back to this page to purchase a license.
